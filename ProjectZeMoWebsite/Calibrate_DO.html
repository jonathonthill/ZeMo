Dissolved Oxygen Calibration:

You may choose between either the Single Point or Dual Point Calibration to calibrate your probe.

Single Point Calibration:

Press the “Single-pt” button.
Remove the cap and discard it. The cap is only used for protecting the probe during shipping.
Expose the probe to air for 5-30 seconds.
Press the “Calibrate” button.
The screen should show “Successful Calibration” otherwise, retry the calibration.

Dual Point Calibration:

Press the “Dual-pt” button.
Remove the cap and discard it. The cap is only used for protecting the probe during shipping.
Expose the probe to air for 5-30 seconds.
Press the “Calibrate” button.
The screen should show “Successful Calibration” otherwise, retry the calibration.
Place the probe in the Dissolved Oxygen solution, and stir to remove trapped air.
Let the probe sit in the solution for 10-90 seconds.
Press the “Calibrate” button.
The screen should show “Calibration Successful” and you will be returned to the main screen for Dissolved Oxygen. If it did not then retry the calibration.
