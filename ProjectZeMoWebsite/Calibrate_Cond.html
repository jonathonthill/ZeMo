Calibrate Conductivity Probe:

Press the “Calibrate” button.
Dry off the conductivity probe.
Press the “Calibrate” button and wait. If the screen should flash a “Calibration Successful” you have successfully done the dry calibration portion. If it did not, then retry the calibration.
Place the probe in the conductivity solution, and stir it to remove trapped air.
Press the “Calibrate” button and wait.
The probe will take readings until it gets within a 40% variance of the solution for two consecutive readings. It will then calibrate the probe, and you should see a “Calibration Successful” blip and be returned to the main screen for Conductivity. If it did not then retry the calibration.
