pH Calibration:

Choose between the single point, two point, or three point calibration.

Single Point Calibration:

Press the “Single-pt” button.
Enter the pH level for the midPt. Should be 7.
Press the “Calibrate” button.
Remove the cap and rinse the probe.
Pour a small amount of solution in the cup.
Place the probe in the solution for 1-2 minutes.
Press the “Calibrate” button.
Do not pour solution back in solution bottle.
The screen should show “Calibration Successful” and you will be returned to the main screen for pH. If it did not then retry the calibration.

Two Point Calibration:

Press the “Two-pt” button.

Press the “Calibrate” button.

Press the “Calibrate” button.
Do not pour solution back in solution bottle.
The screen should show “Calibration Successful”. If it did not then retry the calibration.
